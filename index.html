Master Email Outline

This is the base template for every outreach email. Yellow boxes = fields to fill in. Use the Professor Directory to get pre-filled values for each professor, or use the Email Generator for a complete draft.

SUBJECT LINE
High School Research Interest — [PROFESSOR LAST NAME] Lab / [RESEARCH TOPIC]
EMAIL BODY
Opening / Salutation
Dear [Professor / Dr.] [FULL NAME],
Introduction
My name is [YOUR FULL NAME], and I am a [GRADE, e.g. junior] at Morristown High School in Morristown, New Jersey, where I serve as President of the Engineering Club. I am writing to express my sincere interest in your research at [UNIVERSITY NAME].
Why Their Research / Connection to Paper
I recently came across your work on [SPECIFIC RESEARCH AREA / PAPER TITLE] — specifically, [1-2 sentence description of what about the paper/research interests you and why, in your own words]. This connects directly to [how it connects to your coursework, club projects, or personal engineering interest].
The Ask
I would be honored to contribute to your lab or research group in whatever capacity is most helpful — whether assisting with experiments, data collection, literature reviews, or other tasks. I am genuinely eager to learn, work hard, and contribute meaningfully to the work your team is doing.
Date / Schedule Question
Could you share when your current or upcoming research phase is scheduled, or when a student volunteer might best fit into your lab's timeline? I am available [mention your general availability: e.g. summers, school breaks, weekends] and can adjust around your needs.
Housing (Include if 1+ hour away)
Only include this paragraph if the university is more than ~1 hour from Morristown:

As [UNIVERSITY NAME] is a [approximate drive time, e.g. "roughly two-hour drive"] from Morristown, I wanted to ask whether any student housing, dormitory accommodations, or guest researcher arrangements are available for the duration of participation. If so, I am fully prepared to cover the cost of my lodging myself for the entirety of my stay.
Closing
Thank you very much for your time and consideration. I would be thrilled to speak with you further about how I might be of use to your research. Please feel free to reach me at [YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS] or [YOUR PHONE NUMBER] at your convenience.

With great respect,
[YOUR FULL NAME]
President, Engineering Club — Morristown High School
[YOUR EMAIL]
[YOUR PHONE (optional)]
